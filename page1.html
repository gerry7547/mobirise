Frequently Asked Questions

How to become a Service Provider?

First, you will need to understand what it is to be a UChuuz Service Provider (click here).  Then if you believe u have the skills and the disposition to work as a skilled freelancer, independent contractor and take on on-demand tasks, then fill out the registration form here. 

How do I get work?

Once your registration is approved, we shall send you a link detailing how to create your Uchuuz Service Provider account, set up your bank account details  and complete your profile. Once you complete your account set up,  including creating your Service Provider profile, indicate the area or neighborhood of your jobs preference and your schedule availability so Customers searching for providers on the platform can see it in your profile. If you don’t get tasks or job invitations, then consider adjusting your rates, your work area, adding more availability and additional categories, and editing your profile information to make sure it’s clear to Customers which services you offer. 

How long does it take for my registration application to be processed?

Depending on the volume of applications, it should likely be within 3 working days unless we require more information. Once you received an email that your registration application has been processed, click on a link to set up your UChuuz Account and Profile. 

What experience do I need and how many service categories can I choose? 

You will obviously need the relevant experience when doing a task. Indicating your experience in your profile will be important to customers. Some tasks like errands and deliveries do not require experience.  You may choose to offer more than one category of services. However, it is advisable not to over extend what you can do. Preferably you should focus on what you can do best as customers expect to pay on delivery of service or task promised. 

How do I get paid?

When you set up your provider account, you will have to provide your bank information and billing address in order to get paid via the platform. All your personal and banking information as per PDPA requirements, will be securely kept in strictest confidence and will never be shared with third parties and is for internal use only. 

Do I have to get my own tools? 

Yes, it is important for you to get the proper equipment to get tasks that require tools to get the job done.  For example, if you will be doing delivery or moving bulky items, you will need your own transportation, carts or the appropriate trolleys. If you are doing handyman repair jobs, then you need the proper tools as well.

What fees do I have to pay to be featured on the platform?

There is a registration fee of $25 and a monthly subscription of $25. We will also take 10% commission off the fees payable on the app to you by the customer. The chargeable registration fee is to filter only those who are serious about coming on board with us. The monthly subscription fee is to support our IT infrastructure and operations. The 10% commission should be looked upon as an affordable jobs referral fee.  

How will dispute resolution with customers be handled?

We hope to minimise disputes between service providers and customers. As a service provider, it is important to be transparent and ethical when dealing with customers and managing expectations. Deliver what you have promised. Otherwise make amends as appropriate so you can continue to earn income. 

Can I get others to help me get the task done?

Yes, you can. However, your offered rate should already have accounted for the cost of resources you will be incurring with the additional help. 

What if the task is taking longer than anticipated or there is additional cost due to spare parts?

Customers will be charged your hourly rate upfront.  If the time on the task takes longer than expected, or if there are spare parts costs involved, then you can charge the customer for any incremental costs incurred under "Reimbursements" for the task. However, if it is a packaged offer with no time limit indicated, then you will have to honour the quoted price the customer has agreed to. 

How do I promote my services?

You don't really have too promote your services on the platform if your customer ratings are good. However, you can advertise on the platform. For more details click here.

How do I create a page about my services on the platform?

You can create a paid page to share your services to be provided. For more details click here.
