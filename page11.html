Welcome

We are start-up e-commerce aggregator based in Singapore. Soon we shall start to sign up our array of providers before we launch our various market spaces. In our busy world, with so many things on our plate, UChuuz aims to reduce the hassle with practical and affordable solutions.

Learn more
Who we are

We are a team who view ourselves as enablers bringing all types of equipment & service providers together digitally, then helping to funnel users seeking purchasing alternatives to our providers. 

Unlocking Access & Minimising Hassles

What do we mean by that?

Product Ownership
We understand that product ownership may not be for everyone due to various reasons, but this does not mean that product access cannot be offered affordably and for flexible periods until the user is ready to own the product at his own time or budget.

Services
We also understand the anxiety of trying to get someone to fix stuff that suddenly breaks down on you, like when the plumbing gets broken or the electricals are short circuited.

Personal Development
What about personal development? Where do you find a list of curated personal coaches, gym trainers, good counsellors, skilled tutors, culinary specialists, pastry making courses, and so forth?

Learnings


We at Uchuuz have all had rather similar experiences. Our learnings influenced us to start UChuuz Markets: to unlock users access to the providers, the sellers, the renters, the fixers, the coaches and so many more, all on our meta platform. Ultimately, anyone can go check the providers out and find the appropriate solutionists.

For Providers
This is an opportunity to share the benefits of a digital market space: lower entry barrier with free sign-ups. Once registration is approved, the provider can almost immediately set up shop after some initial training and guidance. We only take a commission from your sales on your gross pricing revenues. Our system will allow providers to decide on their pricing set-up.

We believe its a win/win for all our partners and users.
All sites you create with the Mobirise web builder are mobile-friendly natively. No special actions required. You can preview your site inside the app. Check the icons on the top.

Commitment

We at UChuuz are committed to offering all providers a fair and equitable opportunity to market their product and services competitively to users by leveraging our advanced technology platform and a dedicated account management team.

For users, UChuuz is likewise committed to curate providers and qualify them to join our various spaces. User feedback about the providers on our platform will be very important to us.

Your ratings will help raise standards across the board, and where there are shortcomings, it presents opportunities to continuously learn, improve and do a much better job for all our customers thereafter.
